Soundtrack

The next project the class worked on was creating a sound track. We used Mixcraft which had a variety of different instruments and tracks that we could use. The song had to have a consistent tempo and have all of the different audio files work together on the same beat. This was a difficulty because while the application had a variety of choices one we chose a certain group, it could not be mixed together. I was limited to only 12 instruments to create my sound track. We were required to have panning, proper keyframes, a good balance between tracks and different effects. This was a good project to learn a new software and be able to develop sound tracks on my own.
