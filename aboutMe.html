Robotics

Currently I am in a robotics team where we build a robot and have it compete in an international competition. I was part of the vision sub-team, where we programmed the code for cameras and other sensors to detect the reflective surfaces in the arena. We had a green light on the robot so when the light was reflected on the surfaces we could detect the green. Using the detection we had to determine the distance and angle it was from that target. During the competition the sub-team I was part of was the media team. I was in charge of taking photos of the competition and creating a reveal video for the robot.
