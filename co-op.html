Co-op Projects

LeadFeast Initial Designs

The first project I was given druing my co-op was to create a logo for a company called LeadFeast. I was given the instructions to make it look high tech and the rest I had freedom of choice. I really enjoyed this task becaue it gave me a lot of free range of what I wanted the design to look like and let me creatively think and make my own designs. I created many designs and ran it through my supervisers to determine which would look best for the company.

LeadFeast Final Design

After we determined the base design of the logo I was given the task to create different crations of the logo with different colours for the letters aswell as have different shapes around the logo. I created many different variations of the logo, but my supervisors went with the blue colors and a circle around the logo. I did final adjustments to the design such as making the colour of the full name a lighter blue to stand out more and was then finished with the task.
